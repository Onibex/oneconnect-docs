# OneConnect: Product Overview

## What is OneConnect?

**Onibex OneConnect** is an enterprise-grade integration platform that liberates SAP data and transforms it into real-time, governed Data Products consumable by modern cloud platforms such as **Databricks**, **Snowflake**, and **ClickHouse**.

Unlike traditional ETL pipelines that rely on batch extractions, data duplication, and maintenance-heavy architectures, OneConnect enables enterprises to stream **real-time inserts, updates, and deletes** directly from SAP source systems (ECC, S/4HANA, BW) into downstream analytical and operational systems, without disrupting the SAP Clean Core.

OneConnect is the **only SAP-verified technology** for extracting real-time data and metadata, and it is available in the **SAP Store**, ensuring full compliance with SAP standards for security, scalability, and licensing.

---

## The Problem OneConnect Solves

For decades, SAP integration has been treated as a purely technical exercise: extract, load, transform, repeat. This approach has created several persistent pain points:

| Traditional Pain Point | OneConnect Solution |
|---|---|
| Batch-only extractions cause data staleness | Real-time streaming via SAP standard mechanisms |
| Reconciliation issues when SAP records are modified or deleted | Native INSERT, UPDATE, and DELETE propagation |
| Heavy ABAP development for every integration | Low-code / no-code modeling with visual joins |
| Custom point-to-point integrations multiply maintenance | Reusable Data Products on a governed foundation |
| Long deployment cycles (months) | Pre-packaged Data Products deployable in hours |
| Risk of polluting the SAP Clean Core | External governed data layer preserves SAP integrity |

## The Three Pillars of OneConnect

OneConnect delivers business value through three integrated capabilities:

### 1. Keep Your SAP Clean and Unlock a Trusted Foundation

OneConnect maintains a clean SAP core while creating a **real-time, trusted System of Reference** for all downstream systems, from analytics to AI. Real-time inserts, updates, and deletes ensure that every connected system always reflects the most accurate SAP information, eliminating reconciliation issues and batch-processing delays.

### 2. Operational Intelligence for Everyone

Data-driven decision-making should not be limited to executives. With OneConnect, real-time intelligence flows to every team, from production lines to sales desks, empowering faster, smarter actions across the enterprise.

### 3. Democratized, Hyperconnected, Governed Data Foundation

OneConnect connects SAP transactions with IoT signals, CRM events, and customer interactions in a single **hyperconnected data layer**, providing the trusted foundation that AI, predictive analytics, and operational intelligence require.

## Who Is OneConnect For?

OneConnect is designed for enterprises that:

- Operate SAP ECC, S/4HANA (any version), or SAP BW as core business systems.
- Need to feed modern data platforms (Databricks, Snowflake, ClickHouse) with SAP data.
- Require real-time data freshness for operational intelligence or AI workloads.
- Want to avoid building and maintaining custom point-to-point SAP integrations.
- Need to preserve SAP Clean Core integrity while still exposing SAP data to the broader enterprise.
