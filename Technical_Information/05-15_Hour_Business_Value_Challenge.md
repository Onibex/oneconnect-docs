# The 15-Hour Business Value Challenge

## What Is the 15-Hour Business Value Challenge?

The **15-Hour Business Value Challenge** is a zero-risk, hands-on initiative from Onibex that proves SAP data can be liberated, governed, and made actionable in just **15 working hours**, at no cost to the enterprise.

It is not a demo. It is not a proof of concept. It is a **real-world validation** that SAP data can power a modern cloud Data Warehouse (Databricks or Snowflake) in less than two business days of effort.

---

## What You Get in 15 Hours

In just 15 working hours, Onibex delivers governed, real-time data flows from SAP into Databricks or Snowflake, generating immediate business insights through **pre-packaged Business Data Products**:

### Sales Performance

Real-time visibility into sales orders, billing, customer behavior, and revenue trends, ready for dashboarding and predictive analytics.

### Supply and Demand Inventory Situation

Live reconciliation of stock levels, open orders, and projected demand, enabling proactive supply chain decisions.

### Accounts Receivable Visibility

Real-time AR aging, payment status, and customer credit insights, supporting cash flow management and collections.

### Production Monitoring

Live tracking of production orders, work center capacity, and material availability, enabling shop-floor intelligence.

## Why Just 15 Hours?

Traditional SAP integration projects take months or years because they involve:

1. Custom ABAP development for each extraction.
2. Manual schema mapping and transformation logic.
3. Point-to-point connections that multiply over time.
4. Heavy testing cycles for each integration.

OneConnect collapses this timeline because it eliminates each of those steps:

| Traditional Effort | OneConnect Equivalent |
|---|---|
| Months of ABAP development | Zero ABAP, all visual modeling |
| Manual schema design | Pre-packaged Foundational Data Products |
| Point-to-point integrations | Governed Data Foundation, reusable products |
| Custom CDC implementation | Native real-time INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE |
| Bespoke cloud connector development | Confluent Gold-Verified Premium Connectors |

The 15-hour window is the result of compressing all this work into pre-built, SAP-verified components that simply need to be installed, configured, and pointed at the target cloud platform.
